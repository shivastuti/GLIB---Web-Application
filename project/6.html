GLIB Jornal Search

The GLIB Journal Search enables a user to look for the latest journals in their area of interest.It neatly displays the corresponding fields.I tried bringing in the links to the journals but the API did not support this since most of these had copyrights on them and were not open for masses to view. The GLIB Journal search shows the results in form of pages with 10 results per page. The API does have a very slow speed which results in delay in the display of results.
